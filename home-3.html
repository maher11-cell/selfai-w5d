    § 01

    A quiet manifesto.

    On taste, tools, and the right kind of automation.

    We built W5D because we were tired of AI that demanded our attention more than it earned it. The chatbot, the popup, the badge with a number — small interruptions stacking into a full-time job.

    The work that matters happens in long, quiet stretches. So we made a workspace that disappears when you don't need it, and arrives with answers when you do.

    W5D agents read your sources, draft in your voice, and act inside your tools — but they always show their work. Every claim has a citation. Every action has a log. Every decision is yours.

    "The best AI tool is the one that lets you forget it's there."

    § 02 — Capabilities

    Six tools, one canvas.

    The toolkit is small on purpose. Each piece earns its place.

    01

    Agents

    Multi-step reasoning with tools, memory, and the ability to call each other. Researcher, writer, analyst, support — or build your own.
    02

    Knowledge

    A living index of your team's docs, decisions, and sources. Always cited. Always permissioned. Always up to date.
    03

    Workflows

    Visual chains of agents and actions. Schedule them, trigger them on events, or run them on demand.
    04

    Canvas

    A real-time doc surface where agents and humans co-edit. Comments, presence, version history — like a doc that does the work.
    05

    Connectors

    120+ integrations and a clean API. Bidirectional, with field-level masking and per-action permissions.
    06

    Governance

    SOC 2, SSO, SCIM, granular roles, and a complete audit trail. Compliance teams call it boring, in the best way.
