# 2030B Ecosystem — Projects Exceeding Three Books

## *"Some projects cannot be served by a trilogy. When a dimension is too deep to be reduced, we extend the index — not the talk."*
> — Maher

---

## Why Extend Some Projects?

Extension is not for the sake of adding titles. It is because **some human dimensions naturally branch into applied fields that cannot be merged into a single book without injustice**. Health, for instance, is not one book about the body alone — it is a book for body, a book for soul, and a book for spirit. Honesty is not one book on "do not lie" — it is a book for honesty with self, a book for institutional honesty, and a book for the testimony of truth.
